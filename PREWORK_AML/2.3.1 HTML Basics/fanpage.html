Bio

Nathan Smith, aka NateWantsToBattle, is a musician, voice actor, and YouTuber. He started his journey on Youtube where he eventually caught the eye of thousands! From playing video games to making silly parodies, no matter what he did he was a hit! He has gained an incredible amount of subscribers since he first started, even going over one million on his music channel! Soon enough, he went from making silly parodies to making his own music. In fact, he and his friend Shawn Christmas started their own record label in 2017 known as Give Hearts Records. NateWantsToBattle has become a well known singer since he first started his YouTube channel and for good reason, he makes some really great songs!
